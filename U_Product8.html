Jubilee Worship

Birthed within the heart of Jubilee Christian Church, a breakthrough sound is emanating from the streets of Boston, MA! It is the undeniable sound of unrestrained worship! “It all started when we were much younger ”says songwriter and vocalist Phil Thompson, “When our father (Bishop Gideon Thompson) decided to make the congregation the choir.” The captivating experience that ensued under the direction of Pastor Matthew K. Thompson led to formation of Jubilee Worship. Musically, the quality of the group’s work is undeniable as these worship leaders seamlessly blend well-seasoned vocals with pure worship. Laid over refreshingly original tracks penned by the same writer of Jubilee’s award winning quartet Ashmont Hill, who inspired the world over with hits “Your Masterpiece”, “Praise His Name”, and “Love Lifted Me”, Jubilee’s Worship’s stake throughout the realm of music is much anticipated!
